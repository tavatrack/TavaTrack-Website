How It Works

Set up once. Never miss a call again.

We handle the entire setup. You're live and capturing leads within days.

1

We build your AI receptionist

We send you a short intake form to capture the essentials — your services, service area, pricing approach, and how you like to handle customers. From there, we work with you directly to dial in the details. By the time your AI goes live, it knows your business inside and out.

Your AI answers every call

From the moment it goes live, every call gets answered — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The AI handles questions, captures lead details, flags emergencies, and books appointments directly into your calendar.

The moment a call ends you get a text with the caller's name, number, job description, and a full transcript. No logging in, no checking a dashboard — just a text on your phone with everything you need to follow up.
